About Us

The Special Support Section (SSS) is a department within the Crossbell Police Department dedicated to undertaking tasks that fall outside the purview of traditional law enforcement. Established to address the unique challenges faced by our city, the SSS tackles a wide range of issues, from supporting Bracer operations to conducting independent investigations. Our team, while unconventional, is committed to upholding justice and ensuring the safety of Crossbell's citizens. Regardless of the nature of the request, the SSS is here to serve the community with professionalism and unwavering dedication.

Our Services

Missing Persons & Search & Rescue

Someone gone missing? Don't hesitate to contact us. We'll utilize our network of informants, hidden alleyway connections, and search expertise to bring them home, leaving no stone unturned.

Odd Jobs & Investigations

Need help finding a lost pet, tracking down a wandering debtor, or gathering information on a suspicious character? We'll put our detective skills to work and get to the bottom of it.

Monster Extermination & Threat Mitigation

From rogue Orbal machinations causing citywide blackouts to pesky packs of Wovlverines terrorizing the countryside, we'll handle any monster-related issues that threaten public safety, with a healthy dose of our signature SSS ingenuity.

Community Support & Outreach

The SSS is dedicated to serving the community. We offer educational workshops on safety and self-defense, participate in neighborhood clean-ups, and are always happy to lend a helping hand where needed.
